Suggestions and best Practices

These are Suggestions and Best Practices for submitting Preproposals and Proposals.  If you find new ideas please share them here. With the intention to auto regulate the way the community submit Preproposals and Proposals, a set of recomendations and Best Practices are set in place for:

 

    Guiding about how to prepare the Preproposals and Proposals

    Avoid missunderstandings about general practices

    Prevent abusing the system

 

 

 1 Submittal of Preproposals are encouraged prior to Proposals, input and feedback from the community is encouraged for the good of the project.

2 Preproposals need to be under scrutinity for 6 days before submitting a proposal.

 

3   Budget

 

3.1 The spreadsheet file "Proposal Budget.xls needs to be filled in with real data (sample is attached)

 

3.2 All numbers submitted on the budget are to be in accordance with market prices, high prices on normal items can affect the voting in a negative way, for example 100 USD t-shirts (you get the point).

 

3.3 Submitting bids from vendors is encouraged, filling numbers out of your head without any formal bidding can affect the voting negatively.

 

3.3 Most of costs can be peer reviewd on the Preproposal stage.

 

4. Proposals costs are 100 SWIFTs and 10 SWIFTs once they are approved.(edited)

5. A report is suggested to be filled at the end of the proposal, to have the real prices paid for services to vendors, that way we can save the data for future proposals, as a way of finding real local prices of services and goods.

 

6. If the Proposal is submitted by a group of people, all names have to be included in the proposal, since every one of us will create a reputation within the community.

 

7. Proposals are encouraged to be made for Marketing, Promotion, Development and Support purposes all other initiatives out of the previous mentioned areas must be asked to the community on polls before submitting. We do not say any other proposals can be banned but keep them within the main intention of promotion. Sometimes is good to give to others like helping kids with cancer or so. It will all depend on the community.

 

8. Preproposals for engaging influencers can be submitted.

 

9. Preproposals for Listing on Exchanges can be submitted.

 

10. There will be three basic teams, Development, Outreach and Support which will submit their own proposals, they are not Hives nor have control over the project, but team members who will take care of basic activities, and the community can decide if they continue as per results.

 

11. These recommendations are subject to change or improvement as we all learn how to regulate us in a decentralized way
